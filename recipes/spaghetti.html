Spaghetti

Description

This spicy, pan-fried spaghetti dish is as unusual as it is incredibly delicious. As the head chef, you get to decided exactly how spicy and charred this gets. the sweetness and deliciousness of the mince meat combined with the bolognaise sauce balances the heat from the chili flakes.

The method for this recipe involves boiling the spaghetti for four or five minutes first, then draining, while cooking the mince separately before mixing, pan-frying and saucing for a finished product.

Ingredients

    Spaghetti

  • dry spaghetti
  • salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 litre water
  • 4 tablesoons spaghetti bolognaise sauce

    • Mince meat

  • ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons Chilli flakes
  • 2 teaspoons beef spice
  • 2 teaspoons curry spice
  • 1/4 cup sweet chilli sauce

Steps

    Spaghetti

  1. Add Spaghetti, olive oil, salt and water to a pot
  2. Heat water to boiling point at high heat
  3. Leave the spaghetti to simmer over low heat for 20 minutes
  4. Once the spaghetti has reached the desired texture, drain the remaining water

    5. Mince Meat

  5. Add olive oil to a pan
  6. Fry the onions over medium heat until brown
  7. Add Curry spice to onions
  8. Add ground beef and spices to pan and fry until brown
  9. Pour sweet chilli sauce and bolognaise sauce
  10. Mix cooked spaghetti into ground beef over low heat
  11. Serve!
