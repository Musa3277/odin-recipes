French toast sandwich

Description

This French Toast Sandwich combines the sweetness of two crispy, golden slices of French toast with the savory indulgence of melted cheese, juicy burger patties, and a drizzle of rich sauces. Perfectly balanced with a satisfying crunch, it's served alongside crispy chips for an extra touch of flavor and texture. Ideal for a hearty breakfast, lunch, or dinner, this sandwich is a deliciously unique twist on classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon cooking oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 tomato slice
  • 1 beef pattie
  • 2 cheese slices
  • sauce of choice
  • 1 cup precut fries

Steps

  1. Mix together 2 eggs in a bowl until well combined
  2. Soak 2 slices of bread in the egg mixture
  3. Toast the bread on a pan at medium heat until toasted
  4. Fry the burger patty at medium heat for 3 minutes on each side
  5. Place a slice of cheese on each slice of bread, place a burger patty and an onion topped with your choice of sauces on one slice
  6. Place the other slice of bread and cheese on top of the sandwich
  7. Serve!
