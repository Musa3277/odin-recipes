Description

This delicious chicken wrap features tender, spiced diced chicken strips paired with crisp lettuce, a tangy kick from sweet chili sauce, and a burst of flavor from fresh salsa. Wrapped up in a soft tortilla, it's the perfect balance of savory, spicy, and sweet, making it an ideal meal for lunch or a quick dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • 2 chicken breast fillets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • spices of choice
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons sweet chilli sauce
  • 1000 Islands sauce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
Steps
  1. pour olive oil on a pan at medium heat
  2. Dice the chicken breast fillets and spice as desired
  3. Fry chicken fillets on the pan for 5 minutes, flip and repeat for another 5 minutes
  4. Cut the onion and tomato and mix them into a bowl
  5. Add black pepper spice to the tomato and onion mixture and sauce with sweet chilli sauce
  6. Pour 1000 islands sauce onto each open tortilla in a line at the center
  7. Sprinkle some chicken fillets onto the sauce
  8. Layer the tomato and onions on top of the chiken
  9. Add a little bit of 1000 islands sauce on top of the tomato and onions
    10. \
  10. Fold the tortillas into a wrap
  11. Fry the finished wraps on a pan at medium heat for 1 minute each to seal them
  12. Serve!
